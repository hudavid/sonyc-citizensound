SONYC Tutorial

Hi! This is a demo that shows how you can help us with pattern recognition on sound clips using the PyBossa framework on crowdcrafting.org.

The application is really simple. It loads a soundclip from CUSP and asks you to tag any sounds

All you have to do is to adjust the sliders on the sound visualization and associate time intervals with tags.

Now you are ready to help SONYC!
